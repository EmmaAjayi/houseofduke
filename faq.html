FAQs

Everything you need to know about our process, pricing, measurements, and how to work with House of DUKE Fashion.

The Bespoke Process

What does 'bespoke' mean at House of DUKE?

+

Bespoke at House of DUKE means every garment is made from scratch, specifically for you. It begins with your measurements, your posture, your proportions — and results in a garment that fits and represents you precisely. It is not altered off-the-rack. It is engineered for you.

How does the ordering process work?

+

Our process has five stages: Measurement Consultation → Style & Fabric Refinement → Transparent Price Agreement → Structured Production → Quality Inspection & Delivery. You can start online by submitting the contact form or booking a session, and our team will guide you through each stage professionally.

How does the price negotiation system work?

+

Our negotiation engine is structured and transparent. After you submit your order, we send a formal quotation with the proposed price, timeline, and payment structure. You can accept, or propose a revision. All offers are version-tracked and timestamped. Once both parties agree, the price is locked, an invoice is generated automatically, and production begins. We do not call this bargaining — we call it professional agreement.

What is the payment structure?

+

We operate on a 60% deposit / 40% balance structure. The 60% deposit is required after the price is agreed and the invoice is generated. This activates your production slot. The remaining 40% is due before delivery. Payment options include Paystack and Flutterwave for Nigerian clients, and Stripe for UK and international clients.

How do I submit my measurements?

+

You can submit your measurements directly through the Measurements tab on our Contact page. If you are unsure how to take your own measurements, you can book a virtual consultation via Zoom or WhatsApp and we will guide you through the process. Alternatively, visit us at Suite 17 Salient Plaza, Barnawa, Kaduna for a professional in-person fitting.

How long does production take?

+

Production timelines vary depending on the garment type and complexity. Typical timelines are: Bespoke Suit — 2–3 weeks, Agbada / Senator — 2–3 weeks, Wedding Collections — 3–4 weeks, Corporate Wear — 1–2 weeks. Your exact timeline is agreed and confirmed at the pricing stage, and you can track production progress through your client dashboard once we launch the full platform.

Do you serve clients outside Nigeria?

+

Yes. We proudly serve clients in the UK and internationally. We offer virtual measurement consultations via Zoom or WhatsApp, export-ready garment preparation, and international payment processing via Stripe. You can reach our UK line at +44 754 799 2846.

Can I request a specific fabric or style?

+

Absolutely. At the style and fabric refinement stage, we collaborate with you on your preferred fabric weight, colour, pattern, and structure. You may bring references, describe your vision, or trust our recommendation based on your occasion and climate. Nothing is finalised without your approval.

Can I see examples of your work before ordering?

+

Yes — visit our Collections and Gallery pages to view pieces from our City Royalty, Loud Silence Kaftan, and Urban Culture collections. Every piece shown is available as a bespoke commission tailored to your specifications.

What if I need alterations after delivery?

+

We offer professional alteration services. If any adjustments are required after delivery, please contact us within a reasonable timeframe and we will assess and accommodate the work. Our commitment to your satisfaction does not end at delivery.

How do I reach you directly?

+

You can reach us through multiple channels: WhatsApp NG: +234 803 657 0909, WhatsApp UK: +44 754 799 2846, Instagram: @houseofduke_hod, Facebook Community Group, or by visiting us at Suite 17 Salient Plaza, Barnawa, Kaduna. We respond within 24 hours.

Is my order information kept confidential?

+

Absolutely. We treat all client information — measurements, style preferences, communications, and payment details — with complete discretion and professionalism. Our clients value privacy, and we respond with the same standard.

Still have a question?

Our team is always available to answer any additional questions. Reach us via WhatsApp, Instagram, or submit a message directly.
